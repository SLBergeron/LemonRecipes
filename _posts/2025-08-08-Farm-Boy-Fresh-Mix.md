# 📅 Weekly Meal Plan (Fri → Thu)

## Friday — Wild Rice & Salmon Bowl
**You have:** wild rice *(pantry)*, zucchini *(produce)*  
**Buy:** salmon, lemons, yoghurt, mushrooms  
**Ingredients**
- Wild rice, cooked (1 cup dry → 3 cups cooked)
- Salmon fillet 500 g
- Zucchini 1–2, sliced; Mushrooms 200 g, sliced
- Lemon, Greek yoghurt, garlic *(pantry)*, dill or parsley
**Steps**
1. Cook wild rice (45 min simmer). Season with salt & olive oil.
2. Sear salmon 3–4 min/side; finish with lemon juice & salt.
3. Sauté zucchini + mushrooms in olive oil; season.
4. Sauce: yoghurt + grated garlic + lemon zest/juice + pinch salt.
5. Bowl it up: rice → veg → salmon → drizzle sauce, herbs.

## Sunday — **BBQ Burger Night**
**You have:** spices, eggs, pantry; need buns, beef, cheddar *(have)*  
**Ingredients**
- Ground beef 1 kg; eggs *(pantry)*; salt/pepper; Dijon *(pantry)*
- Burger buns; cheddar slices *(fridge)*; onion, tomato, lettuce *(produce)*
- Side: corn on the cob *(produce)* or potato wedges *(pantry)*
**Steps**
1. Patties: 1 kg beef + 1 egg + 1 tsp Dijon + salt/pepper. Form 6–8 patties.
2. Grill medium-high: ~3–4 min/side (to desired doneness). Rest 5 min.
3. Toast buns; add cheddar to melt on grill.
4. Serve with sliced tomato/onion/lettuce and grilled corn or wedges.

---

## Monday — Sausage & Potato Sheet Pan
**You have:** sausages, potatoes, red onions, garlic *(pantry)*; **Buy:** mushrooms (optional)  
**Ingredients**
- Sausages (4), baby or regular potatoes (cubed), red onion wedges
- Mushrooms 200 g (optional), olive oil, paprika, thyme/oregano
**Steps**
1. Heat oven to 425°F / 220°C.
2. Toss potatoes, onion, mushrooms with oil + paprika + thyme + salt.
3. Add sausages on top; roast 25–30 min until potatoes are tender.

---

## Tuesday — Lemon-Oregano Chicken & Corn + Arugula Salad
**Buy:** chicken thighs, arugula, lemons, cucumber, radishes, feta  
**Ingredients**
- Chicken thighs 4–6; lemon, garlic, oregano, olive oil
- Corn on the cob *(produce)*
- Salad: arugula, cucumber, radishes, cherry tomatoes, feta
**Steps**
1. Marinate chicken 20+ min in lemon juice/zest + garlic + oregano + oil.
2. Grill or roast at 425°F ~25 min (to 74°C/165°F internal).
3. Boil/grill corn. Toss arugula salad with lemon-olive oil dressing; add feta.

---

## Wednesday — Mediterranean Chickpea Salad (Big Batch)
**You have:** dry chickpeas, canned tomatoes *(pantry)*, onions, garlic; **Buy:** olives, roasted red peppers, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, parsley, feta, lemons, tahini  
**Ingredients**
- Cooked chickpeas (soak overnight; simmer 60–90 min with salt)
- Cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley
- Olives, roasted red peppers, feta
- Dressing: olive oil + lemon + oregano; optional tahini-lemon drizzle
**Steps**
1. Combine chopped veg, chickpeas, olives, peppers.
2. Toss with oil, lemon, oregano, salt/pepper. Top with feta.
3. Optional: whisk tahini + lemon + water + salt and drizzle.

---

## Thursday — Panko Chicken Cutlets & Basil-Parmesan Pasta
**You have:** panko, pasta, parmesan, eggs, garlic *(pantry)*; **Buy:** chicken (or use extra thighs), basil, Greek yoghurt (for sauce base)  
**Ingredients**
- Chicken breast or thigh, pounded thin
- Breading: flour → egg → panko; pan-fry until golden
- Pasta; Sauce: sautéed garlic + a spoon of yoghurt + pasta water + parmesan + basil
**Steps**
1. Bread & fry cutlets 3–4 min/side.
2. Cook pasta al dente. Reserve 1 cup water.
3. Sauce: sauté garlic in oil/butter, add yoghurt + splash pasta water, melt in parmesan; toss pasta; fold in basil.
4. Serve with arugula side salad.
